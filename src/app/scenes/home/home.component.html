Our Vision

To upskill, network, & connect the millions of unemployed African college graduates with the job opportunities they need, and provide companies with access and visibility to the high-skilled talent-pool needed to meet their business goals on the continent.

There is a Problem

Sub-saharan Africa (SSA) has one of the world's highest youth unemployment rates. Experts predict roughly 50% of its youth will be unemployed by 2025. What's more, unemployment is highest among university graduates (50% unemployment) even as firms struggle to find high-skilled talent. For graduates, difficulties finding employment detracts from lifetime earnings and entraps them in debilitating poverty. For firms, the labor-imbalance stifles innovation and prevents companies from meeting growth goals.

We have a Solution

Many countries in SSA lack efficient systems for publicizing job opportunities; Moreover, Africa's job market is rife with patronage systems that entrench disadvantage. We will disrupt these inefficient patronage systems by:
CULTIVATE: a vast network of African graduates and early-stage professionals via our superior professional networking platform;
ENHANCE: their employability through access to affordably-priced online certificate courses from globally recognized Ivy-League and Tier-I Universities;
CONNECT: our members with employers through our best-in-class jobs board.

Our Value Proposition...

“TaSA aims to harness the tremendous potential of Africa's brilliant youth for national and continental development by annually connecting 5 million job-seekers with high-skill jobs in and outside SSA by 2040”.
